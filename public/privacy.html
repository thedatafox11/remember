Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Remember ("the app") and the Remember browser extension are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we handle and how.

What we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • Your bookmark library is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. It never leaves your device.
  • Tweet text you save is sent directly from your browser to the Anthropic API for AI classification. This data is not stored on any Remember server.
  • We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking of any kind.
  • We do not create user accounts or collect email addresses.

Browser extension

The Remember Chrome extension reads tweet text and URLs from X/Twitter pages solely to pre-fill the Remember add form. This data is not collected, logged, or shared.

Third party services

Remember uses the Anthropic API to classify and summarise tweet content. Please refer to Anthropic's privacy policy for information on how they handle API data.
